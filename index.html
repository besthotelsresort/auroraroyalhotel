About Aurora Royal Hotel

Fifteen years of checking in, so your trip starts right

Aurora Royal Hotel began in 2011 as a travel column and grew into one of Canada's most-read independent hotel guides. We are not a booking engine and we are not owned by a hotel chain.

Our small team of correspondents pays their own way, stays anonymously wherever possible, and writes about what actually happens between check-in and check-out — the good, the frustrating and the surprising. We never publish prices, we never accept payment for a good word, and every review is signed by the writer who stayed there.

When a link on our site leads you to book a room, we may earn a small commission from the hotel. That keeps our reviews free to read — and it never buys a better score.

Our method

How every Aurora Royal review is made

A consistent, four-step process behind every rating you read.

01

We book & pay

Rooms are reserved like any other guest would — no comps, no "media rate", no advance warning to the hotel.

02

We live in it

Our writer spends at least one full night testing the bed, the shower pressure, the walls, the Wi-Fi and the service.

03

We gather voices

We read hundreds of recent guest reviews to make sure one great (or bad) night doesn't skew the picture.

04

We write it straight

A signed, first-person review with a clear verdict — praise where it's earned, and honesty where it isn't.
